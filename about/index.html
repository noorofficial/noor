Who is Noor?

Noor — known widely as Mr. Noor — is an Indian digital creator and web tools developer from West Bengal, India. His work is rooted in one goal: making the internet more accessible and useful for everyday Indian users.

"Empowering creators with cutting-edge web tools and resources for digital growth."

Since launching mrnoor.in, Noor has built a growing suite of free, mobile-first web tools — from a live age calculator to a study countdown timer — all designed with a distinctive dark space-themed aesthetic and glassmorphism UI that users love.

Beyond tools, Noor is deeply invested in SEO strategy for Indian audiences. He manages Ausgram.com, a project targeting search visibility for Ausgram and related keywords in West Bengal — complete with Schema.org structured data, Bengali-language content, and culturally relevant village data.

Skills & Expertise

Noor works across a wide stack — from front-end HTML/CSS/JS to React and Node.js for web apps, Android Studio for mobile development, and deep expertise in on-page SEO for Indian search audiences. His content work spans both English and Bengali, making him uniquely positioned to serve West Bengal's digital community.

He has also explored real-time applications (WhatsApp-style chat with Socket.io and Firebase), branding design (SVG app icons and brand sheets), and sophisticated WebView-based Android apps with custom JavaScript bridges.
