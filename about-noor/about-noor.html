Who is Noor?

A Digital Creator
Built in India,
Thinking Global

Noor, widely known as Mr Noor, is an Indian digital creator, web developer, entrepreneur, and content strategist rooted in West Bengal, India. He is the founder of mrnoor.in — a platform offering powerful free web tools — and Ausgram.com, an SEO-driven content platform serving Bengali audiences.

Noor's journey is one of grassroots hustle — learning, building, and growing entirely from India. What started as a curiosity for building web tools evolved into a full-scale digital presence spanning multiple platforms, tools, apps, and content formats in both Bengali and English.

As a Noor India brand entity, he has positioned himself as a go-to digital resource for users across India — blending technical depth with cultural relevance. His work reflects a deep commitment to accessibility: free tools, open content, and local-language digital experiences.

About Noor – FAQ

Who is Noor?
Noor, popularly known as Mr Noor, is an Indian digital creator, web developer, and entrepreneur from West Bengal, India. He is the founder of mrnoor.in — a free web tools platform — and Ausgram.com, a local content platform serving Bengali audiences. His work spans SEO, mobile app development, content creation, and brand building.
What does Mr Noor do?
Mr Noor builds free web tools (Age Calculator, Stopwatch, Study Timer, etc.), creates SEO-optimized web pages for Indian audiences, develops Android applications, and produces digital content in both Bengali and English. He also manages personal branding and social media content across ten platforms.
Where is Noor from in India?
Noor is from West Bengal, India. His projects and content are deeply rooted in Bengali culture and geography. He has created content specifically for the Ausgram region of Purba Bardhaman district, reflecting his knowledge of and connection to the local area.
What is mrnoor.in?
mrnoor.in is Noor's primary digital platform offering a suite of free, privacy-friendly web tools including a Live Age Calculator, Income Checker, Events Tracker, Birthday Reminder, Stopwatch, and Study Countdown Timer. The site features a mobile-first, dark space-themed design with glassmorphism UI.
