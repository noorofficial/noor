✦ Who is Noor?

India's Noor — Digital Creator & Web Tools Developer

Noor, popularly known as Mr. Noor, is an Indian digital creator and web tools developer based in West Bengal. With a passion for empowering people online, Noor builds practical, free web tools used by thousands across India.

From age calculators and study timers to SEO-optimized web projects, Noor's work bridges technology and everyday utility. The flagship website mrnoor.in features a suite of free mobile-first tools with a signature dark space-themed design.

Noor is also an Android developer, Bengali-language content creator, and SEO strategist with deep roots in West Bengal's digital ecosystem.
